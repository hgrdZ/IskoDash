How It Works

Order in three steps

01

Browse & Choose

Browse your favorite Lagoon stalls or scroll through your campus feed. Fewa's Footlong, B1T1 Siomai, Kape Kuripot — all at your fingertips.

02

Place Your Order

Add to cart and pay via Cash on Delivery, GCash, or Maya. Your order is broadcast to nearby student riders immediately.

03

Track & Receive

Watch your rider on a live campus map. Get real-time check-in updates and weather alerts until your food arrives at your building.

IskoDash is a student-centered campus delivery platform built by Group 6, BSIT 4-5 at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.

Our Story

A campus problem,
solved by students

Every day at PUP Sta. Mesa, thousands of students face the same struggle: long queues at the Lagoon, limited break time, and the impossibility of getting food between classes without being late.

IskoDash was born from this frustration — a hyper-localized, peer-to-peer food delivery platform designed exclusively for the PUP campus environment.
